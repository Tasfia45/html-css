Tradition of Bangladesh

Bangladesh, a country rich in cultural heritage, boasts numerous traditions that reflect its history, religion, and diverse ethnic communities.



Festivals and Celebrations

  • Pahela Baishakh (Bengali New Year): Celebrated on April 14, it's a vibrant and colorful festival marked by fairs, cultural programs, and traditional foods.

  • Eid-ul-Fitr and Eid-ul-Adha: Major Islamic festivals celebrated with prayers, feasting, and giving to charity.

  • Durga Puja: The biggest Hindu festival in Bangladesh, celebrated with grand decorations and rituals

  • Pohela Falgun (Spring Festival): A celebration of the arrival of spring, characterized by yellow and red attire, flowers, and cultural events.
